## Goals
* Personal blog & branding
* Super simple to browse
* Blazingly fast
* SEO optimized
* 10/10 lighthouse scores
* Easy to turn notion pages into blog posts


## Architecture

* [Qwik](https://qwik.dev) and [Qwik City](https://qwik.city) for components, routing, and SSR/SSG.  
* [Supabase](https://supabase.io) for data storage and authentication.
* [Vercel](https://vercel.com) for hosting and serverless functions.
* [Simple CSS](https://simplecss.org/) to reset my html styles and give me some variables to control my design.

## Target Audience

| Description | Traffic | Interaction type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Other developers interested in the same technology as me | Low with high return rate after interaction on comments etc. | View / comment |
| Businesses or clients looking to for AI / ML skill sets  | Low | View profile and get in touch |
